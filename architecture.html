ARCHITECTURE


The PACE satellite is equipped with a suite of advanced instruments designed to study the ocean's color, aerosols, and clouds.

Ocean Color Instrument (OCI)

The OCI is a hyperspectral radiometer that measures the ocean's color, providing insights into ocean ecology and biogeochemistry.

  • Wavelength range: 350-2500 nm
  • Spatial resolution: 1 km
  • Swath width: 1500 km

SPEXone Polarimeter

The SPEXone is a polarimeter that measures the polarization properties of light scattered by aerosols and clouds.

  • Wavelength range: 400-1700 nm
  • Angular resolution: 0.1°
  • Polarization accuracy: 0.5%

HARP2 Polarimeter

The HARP2 is a polarimeter that measures the polarization properties of light scattered by aerosols and clouds.

  • Wavelength range: 400-1700 nm
  • Angular resolution: 0.1°
  • Polarization accuracy: 0.5%
